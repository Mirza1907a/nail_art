Nail art has evolved into a captivating form of self-expression and creativity within the realm of beauty and fashion. It involves decorating nails with intricate designs, patterns, and colors, often using nail polish, stickers, gems, or other embellishments. 3D Nail Art

This involves creating designs with embellishments like rhinestones, pearls, studs, and tiny charms. These elements are often applied using nail glue and can be shaped and layered to create textured designs.

Basic Manicure and Pedicure

This includes nail trimming, shaping, cuticle care, and moisturizing, often followed by nail polish application. While not exclusively nail art, it forms the foundation for many nail designs.

Nail Art Design

This includes a wide range of designs such as French tips, ombre gradients, geometric patterns, floral motifs, abstract art, and more. Nail artists use nail polish, nail art brushes, stamps, stickers, and other tools to create these designs.

Gel and Shellac Nails

These are types of long-lasting nail polish that are cured under UV or LED light. They provide durability and shine, often used as a base for more intricate nail art designs.
